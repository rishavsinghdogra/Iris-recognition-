IRIS FLOWER CLASSIFICATION

Iris flowers are a diverse and beautiful group of plants known for their striking and intricate blooms. They belong to the genus Iris, which is part of the family Iridaceae. There are over 300 different species of iris, and they are distributed throughout the world, with a concentration in temperate regions. These flowers have been cultivated for centuries and have a rich history in various cultures.


Iris flowers are not only prized for their ornamental value but also for their symbolism in various cultures. They are often associated with meanings such as wisdom, faith, and hope. Irises are also used in perfumery and have medicinal properties in some traditional herbal remedies. Whether you're a gardener, a flower enthusiast, or simply appreciate their beauty, irises offer a wide range of options to enjoy and explore.
